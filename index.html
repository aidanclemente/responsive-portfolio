About Me

My name is Aidan Clemente. I was adopted from South Korea when I was 13 months old. I grew up the youngest of eight children in State College, Pennsylvania. After graduating from Pennsylvania State University with my B.S. in Criminal Jusitce I moved to Pittsburgh, PA. I attended Chatham Univeristy where I earned my Masters in Education. I took a job teaching high school mathematics at Colorado Timberline Academy (CTA) in Durango, Co and promptly moved.


I resided in Colorado for six years before moving to Florida to be closer to family and pursue formal education in computer coding. Ideally, my switch into coding will utilize my past education, mathematical skills and teaching background.
